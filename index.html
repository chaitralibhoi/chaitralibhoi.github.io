About

Chaitrali Bhoi is Research Associate at the Center for Digital Public Goods, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, India. Her areas of research include Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI), Data Policy, Protocols and Networks, Sustainability and Carbon Market. Her work includes contributions to data management policy, leveraging protocols and open networks to design scalable DPI systems, and context-specific DPI implementation.
